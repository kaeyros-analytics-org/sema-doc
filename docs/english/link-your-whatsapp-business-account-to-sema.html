What is a WhatsApp Business account?

A WhatsApp Business account is a WhatsApp account designed specifically for your business , as opposed to the traditional WhatsApp account you probably use with friends and colleagues.

You can manage your WhatsApp Business account from the WhatsApp Business Manager , which allows you to access your WhatsApp Business account information, settings, notifications, messages and phone numbers.
